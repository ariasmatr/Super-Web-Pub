Since
1989

Salamanca,
Madrid

In the heart of Madrid's exclusive Salamanca neighborhood lies The Mirage, a venue designed to redefine luxury and entertainment. Since its opening in 1989, this iconic space has been much more than just a bar or nightclub; it is a must-visit destination for those seeking a unique and sophisticated experience. Renowned for its elegance and avant-garde ambiance, The Mirage has been featured in prestigious publications such as Vogue Magazine and has established itself as the go-to venue for exclusive events in the capital.

Famous for attracting international artists, celebrities, and influential figures, its stage has witnessed unforgettable nights where live music and renowned DJ sets set the tone. By day, it transforms into a haven for brunches and private gatherings, while at night, its vibrant energy and curated selection of signature cocktails make it the epicenter of Madrid's glamour.

By day, a sophisticated haven.
By night, the epicenter of entertainment.
